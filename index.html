数学与周易的交汇 / Mathematics Meets I Ching

64卦与二进制完美对应：每卦六爻（阴=0，阳=1），从 000000 到 111111，对应十进制 0~63。莱布尼茨受周易启发创立二进制。

斐波那契数列（黄金螺旋）与周易卦序、自然生长模式高度契合。

The 64 hexagrams correspond perfectly to binary: 6 lines (Yin=0, Yang=1) map to 000000–111111 (0–63). Leibniz was inspired by the I Ching to develop binary system.

Fibonacci sequence (golden spiral) resonates deeply with I Ching hexagram order and patterns of natural growth.

晶体结构与能量载体 / Crystal Structures & Energy Carriers

蜂巢六边形是自然界最稳定的结构之一。晶体通过规则的分子晶格，成为能量的优良载体。声音振动能影响晶体形成（Cymatics现象），被视为“冻结的音乐”。

Hexagonal honeycomb is one of nature’s most stable structures. Crystals act as powerful energy carriers through their ordered lattice. Sound and vibration shape crystal formation (Cymatics), known as “frozen music”.

振动、频率与声音 / Vibration, Frequency & Sound

一切皆振动。不同频率的声音可与晶体、人体能量场产生共振。周易的阴阳、二进制的0与1、晶体的几何，皆指向宇宙统一的频率语言。

Everything is vibration. Sound frequencies resonate with crystals and human energy fields. Yin-Yang of I Ching, 0/1 of binary, and crystal geometry all point to the universal language of frequency.
